Connections

How did I start playing this game?

The first time I heard about Company of Heroes 2 was when it was featured in the steam store for being free for a limited time. I thought "why not" and added it to my library. It sat there collecting dust for about half a year until one day I decided to play it. I was immediately enthralled by the stratagy and fast paced gameplay. To this day I still play Company of Heroes 2 actively.


How much do I like this game?

Company of Heroes 2 is my favourite game. It is my third most played game (behind Unturned and Team Fortress 2) but it is also the only one that I still play actively out of my top 3 most played games.


Is it worth it?

I think the base game is definitely worth it. It costs $21.99 CAD on steam. This comes with the multiplayer feature, two starting factions (Soviet and Wehrmancht), and the main campaign. The other three factions costs around $34 CAD. I would not recomend buying those DLCs at full price. What I like about this game is the fact that the DLCs are not necessary and will not diminish your enjoyment.
