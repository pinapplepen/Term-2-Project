Company of Heroes 2

Company of Heroes 2 is a real-time strategy video game made by Relic Entertainment. It is the sequel to the game Company of Heroes. The game takes place in the European Theater of WW2 and lets you play as the Wehrmacht or Soviets in the base game.

Campaign

The campaign is centered around the memories of a Soviet lieutenant named Lev Abramovich Isakovich. The story starts with Lt. Isakovich in a gulag for trying to defect from the army. Then his former commanding officer, Colonel Churkin, comes in and interrogates him. Isakovich then recounts his experiences to explain why he defected. His flashbacks are the missions that you play. Once you have finished all the missions (spoilers ahead)...
you are sent back to reality in the gulag and the executioner comes in. He prepares to execute Lt. Isakovich but is shot by Colonel Churkin. Churkin then reveals to Isakovich that he would not survive the next purge and helps him escape the gulag to tell the world about the horrible atrocities commited during WW2.
