Gameplay

Introduction

Company of Heroes 2 provides an unique individual squad control experience that requires intensive micromanaging and stratagizing. It also has an interesting cover system which will provide the squad with buffs or debuffs depending on what cover they have. Most cover is directional so it promotes flanking and suprise attacks.







The entire game revloves around Territory Points. These are points that, if captured, will provide a steady stream of resources or buffs. There are 5 types of territory points in the game, Normal, Fuel, Munition, Fuel, Medical, and Victory. Most of the other points are self explanitory but Victory points are different. You can achieve victory by holding on to them for a certain amount of time. But remember, enemies can steal any point you caputre.







Many commanders have powerful abilities they can use in exchange for munitions. Some examples are artillery barrages, air strikes, incendiary bombs, smoke drops, etc.
